# 🏥 The Stroke Awareness Story: A Teammate-Friendly Summary

This is the behavioral "map" of our population. We didn't just find clusters; we found **people** and the gaps in their health habits.

---

## 🔬 How we built the story
We used **5 different scientific models** to ensure our groups weren't accidents. We cleaned the data, tested for statistical significance (**p < 0.001**), and triangulated our results to ensure **accuracy**.
